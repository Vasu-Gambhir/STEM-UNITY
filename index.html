About Us

Who are we?

The increasing lack of STEM education for children worldwide is problematic because it limits their opportunities to acquire essential skills for the modern world. This deficit hinders their ability to participate in technological advancements, pursue STEM-related careers, and contribute to global innovation. As technology continues to advance, this gap could lead to greater inequalities and hinder the progress of societies on a global scale.Therefore, STEM UNITY strives to tackle and bring about positive changes in the deficiency of STEM education.

Importance of STEM Education

Lack of STEM education hampers readiness, limits tech involvement, and stifles innovation.

Disparity and Inequality Impact

Tech skills gap widens inequalities, hindering global progress.

Tech Progress Hurdle

Weak STEM foundation obstructs innovation and adaptation.

Role of STEM UNITY

STEM UNITY empowers kids for tech-driven progress.

Our Mission

STEM UNITY strives to enhance STEM education for underprivileged children, with the aim of creating a positive impact on a global scale.

Our Story

In early 2023, I was on a trip to India where I came across a group of underprivileged kids who were living on the side of the streets. I was unable to get in contact with them directly, but from afar I was able to observe how they didnâ€™t go to school on a normal school day and lacked sources of education. This brought back memories from when I was younger and would often see my mom donate books and educational materials to orphanages. I had always had a strong passion for STEM subjects and always wanted to pursue something in the field. Remembering those times and the stories I heard inspired me to start something called STEM UNITY. A STEM based organization which conducts workshops to educate underprivileged kids.
